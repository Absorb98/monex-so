Bizad

The core concept for Biz Ad is to create an eco-system that enables businesses and consumers to connect, the platform would allow 3 different registration categories i.e Basic (For users & personal businesses), Boomin (For businesses) & Bringer (For the delivery personnel) that would enable each of these user categories to create a source of revenue.

Oomco

OOMCO WORLD was envisioned for facilitating consumers with ease and better user experience when using Oman Oil station’s services. The idea was to offer a better experience in terms of refueling, pre-order for snacks/coffee from onsite shops with seamless payments while planning their journey. The idea entailed including GPS and AI-based promotions, discounts, and rewards to be redeemed later.


When built, the app proved beneficial with all its features including discounted services available at the Oman Oil stations along with additional loyalty benefits.

Curious

This solution was envisioned based on the struggles people experience with anxiety when posting on social platforms. The aim was to create a revolutionary app that would offer users complete anonymity when expressing themselves. The app is built to offer users the latest updates and the nearest trendy locations amid a phenomenal experience.


The result achieved was an intuitive UI with easy navigation, offering anonymity, with separate logins and profiles, customizable feed, and the ability to explore shared interests.

HotSet

HOTSET was envisioned as a real time Covid testing and tracking system. This idea was particularly formulated to cater to production houses. The team working on this project was tasked with conducting extensive research that would enable them to set the benchmarks for the app. After that, there was the development process that would span several weeks to deliver a complete product.

ApplyUp

Apply Up has many robust recruitment and job search features, algorithms, and unique mechanics. Apply Up works by directly matching employers and job seekers. Job seekers easily apply to any job posted by employers by easily swiping right on a job post in our explore, search, and filter features. This feature is a proprietary solution that was carefully crafted for the most successful matching experience.
